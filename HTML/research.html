Research Part-1

Product Component

Here, we used the asos shopping website as a reference to create a similar product component. In our product component we have added an 'add to card' button. There is also an alert popup when you press the add to cart button notifiying that the item has been added to cart.

Research Part-2

Feedback Form

Here, we took inspiration from the Healthy Kitchen page for our feedback section. We adjusted the styling by making the background color transparent and applying a backdrop filter blur of 20px. Additionally, we enhanced the submit button styling and incorporated an alert message. If any field is left empty, it displays a message saying 'Please fill in all the fields.' On successful completion of all fields, a 'Thank you for your feedback' message is shown.

Research Part-3

Footer

Our footer section is inspired by ASOS. We aimed to replicate the styling of its footer, making adjustments to colors and content to better suit our design and requirements.

Research Part-4

Navigation bar

While browsing various websites, the navigation bar of the Pancbike website caught our attention. We tried to replicate it on our page, making adjustments to the logo, background color, font style, and content to meet our specific requirements.

Research Part-5

Go To Top Button

As we were navigating through various websites to take inspiration from, We came across the WWF's website and we liked their Go to Top button. We modified the styling by adding the upwards arrow and the text 'top'. For the scrolling mechanism we used CSS instead of JS.
